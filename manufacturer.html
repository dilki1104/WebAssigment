Description

Established by Merrill J. Fernando, now the world's most experienced teamaker, the Sri Lankan family tea company Dilmah has championed consistency, authenticity and variety in tea. In 1988, when the family business went against industry trends to announce its devotion to authenticity, Dilmah pioneered the Single Origin Tea idea. A hallmark of Dilmah, garden fresh, unblended tea offers a unique taste of unblended Ceylon tea packed at the source.

History

As he saw the accumulation of ownership in the tea industry in the hands of a few major companies in the 1950s, Merrill J. Fernando devoted his life to tea. This has led to tea commoditisation. He determined that he would counteract this trend of commoditisation in the interest of tea drinkers around the world, and the crop that his country created with so much care and artistry. This took him about four decades and in 1988 he finally launched his own brand â€“ Dilmah. The company was the first tea brand owned by a retailer, and originally offered tea 'picked, perfected and packed.' Unlike most of the tea blends that dominated supermarket shelves, the tea that is freshly packed at source and hence rich in flavor and goodness making it one of the finest teas of the world.

The head of the Richy Group of Companies is Mr. Wirantha Hettiarachchi, Chairman / Managing Director. He has had extensive exposure to the tea trade over the last 15 years and has expertise in areas such as Tea Tasting, Tea Processing, Tea Blending, Flavored Tea Processing & Trading. History

In 1991, founder, Mr. Wirantha Hettiarachchi, believed in offering all Sri Lankans the finest cup of tea and began production of their own tea. As time passed, the operations reached new heights, giving them the opportunity to start its own marketing and distribution operation called Green Island Marketing Services, Now Richy has sucessfully become the 3rd largest locally owned brand in the country. It now has its own vehicle fleet and sales force serving all parts of the country, from North to South and East to West, selling to over 28,000 island-wide outlets.

Description

Minah Tea Exports (Pvt) Ltd was established in 2012 with the vision of exporting Pure Ceylon Tea to tea enthusiasts around the world by venturing to export Ceylon black tea from its wholly-owned tea estate located in southern Sri Lanka and to deliver excellence in local and international marketing of our own tea brands. In order to quench the global thirst for tea with the perfect blend of Pure Ceylon Tea, Minah Tea ensures that the high quality of Ceylon black tea or green tea is maintained by combining only the best tea leaves grown and produced in its own gardens.

History

Minah Group founded in 1989 as a family business mainly focused on tea growing and manufacturing, has since been catering Pure Ceylon Tea to global tea consuming markets. The Minah Community is able to produce 100,000 kgs per month of low-grown orthodox teas. Minah Tea is the home brand of Minah Group of Companies, a vertically integrated company in the tea industry with growing, processing, branding, and marketing of tea as its main focus.
